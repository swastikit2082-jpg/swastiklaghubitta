हाम्रो लक्ष्य

गरिबी निवारण र आर्थिक समावेशीकरणमा योगदान पुर्याउनु हो। हामी साना व्यवसायी, किसान र महिला उद्यमशीलहरूलाई सहज ऋण सुविधा प्रदान गरी आत्मनिर्भर बनाउन प्रतिबद्ध छौं।

🔭

हाम्रो दृष्टि

मधेश प्रदेशको अग्रणी लघुवित्त संस्था बन्नु हो। हाम्रो दृष्टि भनेको प्रत्येक ग्राहकलाई गुणस्तरीय वित्तीय सेवा प्रदान गर्दै उनीहरूको जीवनमा सकारात्मक परिवर्तन ल्याउनु हो।

💎

हाम्रा मूल्यहरू

इमान्दारी, पारदर्शिता, ग्राहक केन्द्रित सेवा र सामाजिक उत्तरदायित्व हाम्रा मूल्य हुन्। हामी प्रत्येक ग्राहकलाई परिवारको रूपमा हेर्छौं र उनीहरूको सफलतामा आफ्नो सफलता देख्छौं।

👔 Dhirendra Lal Karn | Chief Executive Officer

Namaskar, Swastik Microfinance Financial Institution Ltd., promoted by local personalities of Madhesh Province, has been licensed by Nepal Rastra Bank as a "Class D" (provincial level) financial institution and has been providing financial services since Chaitra 12, 2075 B.S.


The objective of this financial institution is to provide financial services through the microfinance program to the people of this province, to safeguard savings, and to encourage youths who wish to engage in business within their own country to become self-employed through collateral or collateral-free loans.


We express our heartfelt gratitude to all the respected shareholders, customer members, employee colleagues, regulatory bodies, Board of Directors, and all well-wishers who have directly and indirectly provided support and cooperation to this financial institution.
